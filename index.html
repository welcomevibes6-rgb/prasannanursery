ABOUT SATYA PRASANNA NURSERY

20+ Years of Experience in
Fruit Plants & Nursery Care

Satya Prasanna Nursery is a trusted destination for fruit plants and garden varieties, known for maintaining healthy and high-quality plants. The nursery is proudly managed by farmer P. Chellaya (Bujji), who has more than 20 years of experience in cultivating and caring for fruit plants.

Our nursery is widely recognized for offering over 199+ varieties of fruit plants, carefully grown and maintained to ensure strong growth and high productivity. With deep agricultural knowledge and practical experience, we guide plant lovers, farmers, and home gardeners in selecting the right plants for their gardens and farms.

At Satya Prasanna Nursery, our mission is to bring nature closer to every home by providing healthy plants, reliable guidance, and sustainable growing practices.
